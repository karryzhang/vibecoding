🧠 三层功能架构 🧠 Three-Layer Feature Architecture

Layer 1：看懂层 — AI 帮你分析

Layer 1: Understanding — AI Analyzes For You

「交易前，先问 TradeAI」

"Before trading, ask TradeAI"

  • Token 快速分析：输入任意币，AI 一句话总结「什么情况」
  • Quick Token Analysis: Enter any token, AI summarizes "what's the situation" in one line
  • 链上透视：聪明钱流向、内部人买卖、持仓集中度
  • On-chain X-ray: Smart money flow, insider trades, holder concentration
  • 风险雷达：合约风险评分、Rug 概率、社交异常
  • Risk Radar: Contract risk score, Rug probability, social anomalies
  • 市场情绪：Twitter/Telegram 情绪分析 + KOL 观点汇总
  • Market Sentiment: Twitter/Telegram sentiment + KOL opinion summary

Layer 2：建议层 — AI 帮你判断

Layer 2: Advisory — AI Helps You Decide

「不确定的时候，TradeAI 给你建议」

"When uncertain, TradeAI gives advice"

  • 个性化建议：基于你的持仓 + 风险偏好，给出「现在应该怎么做」
  • Personalized Advice: Based on your holdings + risk preference, "what you should do now"
  • 仓位提醒：「你的 XX 占比过高，建议分散」
  • Position Alerts: "Your XX allocation is too high, suggest diversifying"
  • 时机提示：「ETH 跌到 $2800 可以考虑加仓」（你自己决定是否操作）
  • Timing Tips: "Consider adding when ETH drops to $2800" (you decide whether to act)
  • 风险预警：「检测到异常，建议立即检查」
  • Risk Warnings: "Anomaly detected, recommend checking immediately"

Layer 3：执行层 — AI 帮你操作（需授权）

Layer 3: Execution — AI Operates For You (Requires Auth)

「说一句话，TradeAI 帮你搞定」

"Say one sentence, TradeAI handles it"

  • 意图执行：「帮我买 500U ETH，分两次买」→ AI 规划 + 你确认 → 执行
  • Intent Execution: "Buy $500 ETH for me, in two batches" → AI plans + You confirm → Execute
  • 条件单：「如果跌到 $2700 帮我加仓」→ 设置后自动监控和执行
  • Conditional Orders: "Add position if it drops to $2700" → Set up auto-monitoring and execution
  • 智能路由：AI 自动选最优 Gas、最小滑点路径
  • Smart Routing: AI auto-selects optimal gas, minimum slippage path
  • 权限控制：用户设置单笔上限、日上限、可操作币种白名单
  • Permission Control: User sets per-trade limit, daily limit, token whitelist
⚠️ 关键设计：执行层是「可选开启」的高级功能，默认关闭。用户必须主动授权、设置限额后才能使用。AI 永远不会在未授权情况下操作用户资产。 ⚠️ Key Design: Execution layer is an "opt-in" advanced feature, off by default. Users must actively authorize and set limits before use. AI will never operate user assets without authorization.
