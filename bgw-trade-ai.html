🧠 产品功能架构 🧠 Product Feature Architecture

Layer 1：信息层 — AI 帮你看懂

Layer 1: Information — AI Helps You Understand

  • Token 智能分析：输入任意币，一句话总结「值不值得买」
  • Token Smart Analysis: Enter any token, get one-line verdict on "worth buying or not"
  • 链上透视：聪明钱流向、内部人买卖、巨鲸持仓变化
  • On-chain X-ray: Smart money flow, insider trades, whale position changes
  • 风险雷达：合约漏洞、Rug 风险评分、社交异常检测
  • Risk Radar: Contract vulnerabilities, Rug risk score, social anomaly detection
  • 实时舆情：Twitter/Telegram 情绪分析 + KOL 动向追踪
  • Real-time Sentiment: Twitter/Telegram sentiment + KOL movement tracking

Layer 2：决策层 — AI 帮你判断

Layer 2: Decision — AI Helps You Decide

  • 个性化建议：基于你的持仓 + 风险偏好 + 市场状态，给出具体操作建议
  • Personalized Advice: Based on your holdings + risk preference + market state, give specific action advice
  • 仓位管理：「你现在应该卖掉 30%，理由是…」
  • Position Management: "You should sell 30% now, because..."
  • 时机提醒：「ETH 跌到 $2800 时建议加仓」
  • Timing Alerts: "Suggest adding when ETH drops to $2800"
  • 组合优化：「你的仓位过于集中 Meme，建议分散」
  • Portfolio Optimization: "Your position is too Meme-heavy, suggest diversifying"

Layer 3：执行层 — AI 帮你做

Layer 3: Execution — AI Does It For You

  • 意图交易：「帮我买 1000U ETH，分 3 次，每跌 2% 买一次」
  • Intent Trading: "Buy $1000 ETH for me, in 3 batches, buy each time it drops 2%"
  • 智能路由：自动选择 Gas 最低、滑点最小的执行路径
  • Smart Routing: Auto-select path with lowest gas and slippage
  • 跨链执行：「把 Base 上的 USDC 换成 Solana 上的 SOL」
  • Cross-chain Execution: "Swap USDC on Base to SOL on Solana"
  • 条件单：止盈止损、价格触发、时间触发
  • Conditional Orders: Take-profit/stop-loss, price triggers, time triggers
