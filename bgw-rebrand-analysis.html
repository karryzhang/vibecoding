01 — 执行摘要

渐进式品牌演进，条件成熟后 Rebrand Progressive Evolution, Rebrand When Ready
Rebrand 战略方向正确，但时机未到。当前应聚焦产品消费化，同步进行品牌刷新，当触发条件满足时再启动正式更名。 Rebrand direction is correct, but timing is premature. Focus on product consumerization now, with brand refresh, then formal rebrand when triggers are met.
核心洞察
Core Insight

品牌是产品的影子，不是产品的先导。 Rebrand 成功的前提是产品已经「变了」，名字只是让外界「认识到」这个变化。Meta 的失败正是因为「改名在前，产品在后」。

Brand is product's shadow, not its precursor. Successful rebrand requires product to have "changed" — name just makes the world "recognize" this change.

04 — 历史案例研究

成功案例

Success Cases

TransferWise → Wise
2021 · Full Rebrand
产品已从「汇款」扩展到「多币种账户、借记卡」，旧名字限制认知。先有产品扩展，后有名字变更。
Product expanded from "transfers" to "multi-currency accounts, debit cards." Product expansion first, then name change.
Square → Block
2021 · Corporate Rebrand
业务包含 Cash App、比特币，「Square」只代表 POS。母公司换名，子品牌保留。
Business included Cash App, Bitcoin — "Square" only represented POS. Parent renamed, sub-brands retained.
Klarna
2020 · Brand Refresh
不是改名，而是品牌刷新。通过大胆营销打破金融「无聊」印象。保留名字，改变感觉。
Not renaming, but brand refresh. Bold marketing broke "boring finance" image. Keep name, change feeling.

失败案例

Failure Cases

Facebook → Meta
2021 · Full Rebrand
改名在前，愿景在后。元宇宙产品还没做出来。被认为是「逃避丑闻」。公众信任度下降 5%。
Name changed before vision realized. Metaverse product not ready. Seen as "escaping scandal." Public trust dropped 5%.

"Facebook 的 Rebrand 有一个根本性问题：它在愿景成为现实之前就改名了。"

"Facebook's rebrand has a fundamental problem: it rebranded before its vision is reality."

— Harvard Business Review, 2021

05 — 品牌架构选项

A — House of Brands（独立品牌）
Alphabet → Google/YouTube · P&G → Tide/Gillette
创建全新品牌名，与 Bitget 无直接关联。最大品牌塑造空间，但从零建立认知，成本最高。适用于当 Bitget 品牌对目标用户有负面联想时。
Create entirely new brand, no Bitget connection. Maximum brand building space, but start from zero, highest cost. Use when Bitget has negative association.
B — Endorsed Brand（背书品牌）
Trust Wallet by Binance · Marriott Courtyard
创建新品牌名，但标注 "by Bitget"。独立身份 + 母品牌信任背书，两全其美。需要平衡两个品牌关系。
Create new brand, but note "by Bitget". Independent identity + parent trust endorsement. Need to balance two brand relationships.
C — Branded House（品牌延伸）
Google Pay · Apple Watch
保留 Bitget 前缀，如 "Bitget Pay"。保留现有品牌资产，降低重建成本。但难以突破「交易所钱包」印象。
Keep Bitget prefix, like "Bitget Pay". Retains brand equity, lower rebuild cost. But hard to break "exchange wallet" impression.
D — 渐进式演进（推荐）
TransferWise → Wise
先保持现名，聚焦产品消费化 + 品牌刷新。当触发条件满足时，再启动正式 Rebrand。风险最低，有数据验证，可随时调整。
Keep current name, focus on product consumerization + brand refresh. Formal rebrand when triggers met. Lowest risk, data-validated, adjustable.

总结 Summary

Rebrand 是正确的战略方向，但时机是关键。

Rebrand is the right direction, but timing is key.

当前建议：产品先行 → 品牌刷新 → 验证触发条件 → 再决策更名。学习 Wise 的成功路径：用产品变化赢得用户，用品牌变化让世界认识到这个变化。

Current recommendation: Product first → Brand refresh → Validate triggers → Then decide. Learn from Wise: Win users with product change, let the world recognize this change with brand change.
